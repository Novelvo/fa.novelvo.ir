About

Novelvo is a place for people to read about people, places, movies and so many other things.
Also Novelvo has downloading features with certificated license.

Novelvo is working under Neotrinost LLC.
Founded by Amirhossein Mohammadi and Annahita Mirhosseini.


Why Novelvo?

Novelvo is Open Source. All the subdomains are a repository in Github.
In Novelvo we have No Ads. We support many languages. English, Persian, German and French.

We support developers. People who help in developing, finding problems and others who contribute!
Simple. Fork, Clone, Add, Commit, Push and Pull Request!


Contacting

Novelvo just has Telegram channel. There is no Instagram or other pages.
As we said, we have Github page the there are all of our projects.

We are open 27/7. You can call, ask and tell us if you have any problem in Novelvo.
